Personalized Birthday Cakes

User functions
-----------------------
1. Log in and log out
2. Registration providing name, address, and payment type
3. Order tracking
4. Cake can be picked up from store or delivered during order checkout

Admin functions
-----------------------
1. Orders can be fulfilled and managed
- Order table will contain orders placed by multiple customers and a customer can place more than one order

2. Inventory tracking

Core app functionality
-----------------------
1. Cakes based on size:
 - One person personal cake
 - Medium cake for several people
 - One large cake for many people

2. Flavors:
 - Vanilla with colorful sprinkles
 - Chocolate cake
 - Fruit cake option

3. Cake decoration:
 - Personalized with age and name

4. Cake can be assigned a date of manufacture
